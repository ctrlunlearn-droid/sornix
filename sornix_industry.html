Who We Are

Sornix is a digital automation studio that designs and deploys intelligent automation systems for social media management, AI content generation, image processing, and digital workflow automation. Our tools connect with Telegram, Pinterest, Cloudinary, Anthropic, ImgBB, and Make.com.

// 02

Information We Collect

We minimize data collection. We may process:

  • Images — photos submitted for processing. Processed in transit, not stored by Sornix.
  • API credentials — stored only in your own automation environment.
  • Telegram IDs — used solely to send confirmations back to you.
  • Operational metadata — timestamps within third-party platforms only.

We do not collect names, emails, payment info, location, or any PII through our tools.

// 03

How We Use Data

All data is used exclusively to deliver the requested automation:

  • Process, style, and optimize images as instructed
  • Generate AI captions, descriptions, and hashtags
  • Publish content to your connected platforms
  • Send confirmation notifications via Telegram
  • Operate and improve our systems
// 04

Data Sharing & Third Parties

Your data may pass through these services as part of normal automation:

  • Make.com — automation orchestration
  • Cloudinary / ImgBB — image processing and hosting
  • Anthropic / Claude AI — content generation
  • Pinterest — publishing
  • Telegram — notifications

Sornix does not sell, rent, or share your data for commercial purposes.

// 05

Data Storage & Security

Sornix tools are stateless by design — no central database of your content.

✓ Your content lives in your accounts: Cloudinary, ImgBB, Pinterest. Sornix holds no copies.

API keys are stored in your own automation environment (e.g., Make.com) and never transmitted to Sornix.
